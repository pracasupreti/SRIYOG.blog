What is zoho recruit?

3 Mins Read
Prakash Upreti
30 July, 2024

Zoho Recruitment is a cloud-based recruitment software that helps businesses streamline and automate their hiring process. It is part of Zoho’s suite of business tools and is designed to assist HR teams and recruiters in managing the entire recruitment workflow, from posting job openings to hiring the right candidates

Key Features of Zoho Recruit:

Here are some of the key features of Zoho Recruitment:

  • Customizable Job Posting:
    You can create job listings, customize them as needed, and post them on various job boards, social media platforms, and your company’s website. This feature helps you reach a larger pool of candidates.
  • Candidate Sourcing
    Zoho Recruit enables recruiters to source candidates from multiple channels such as job boards, LinkedIn, and internal candidate databases. It also allows you to import resumes and parse them for key information.
  • Applicant Tracking
    The platform helps you track candidates through each stage of the recruitment process. You can view their applications, track progress, and keep records of interactions. It allows you to move candidates through stages like initial screening, interviews, and final selection.
  • Resume Parsing
    Zoho Recruit offers a resume parsing feature, which automatically extracts key information from resumes and inputs it into the system, making it easier to assess candidates quickly.
  • Collaboration Tools
    The software offers features that make collaboration easy within hiring teams. Team members can provide feedback on candidates, schedule interviews, and communicate through the platform.
  • Interview Scheduling
    Zoho Recruit allows recruiters to schedule interviews with candidates, send invitations, and even sync interviews with calendar apps like Google Calendar. It helps ensure that no interviews are missed and the process is efficient.
  • Customizable Workflows
    You can customize workflows according to your hiring process. This flexibility allows you to streamline the recruitment steps, tailor the system to your specific needs, and automate repetitive tasks.
  • Candidate Database
    A central database stores all your candidate information in one place. You can search for candidates, view their profiles, and access any previous interactions or feedback. It makes managing your talent pool easier and more organized.

Benefits of Zoho Recruit:

  • Time-saving
    Automates manual tasks such as resume screening, interview scheduling, and follow-ups, giving you more time to focus on critical recruitment tasks.
  • Better Candidate Experience:
    With its user-friendly interface and smooth application process, candidates have a better experience applying for jobs.
  • Improved Hiring Efficiency:
    By offering tools like resume parsing, interview scheduling, and reporting, Zoho Recruit ensures a faster and more efficient hiring process.
  • Team Collaboration:
    Zoho Recruit's team collaboration features make it easy for multiple team members to work together in evaluating candidates and making hiring decisions.
  • Scalable:
    Whether you're a small business or a large enterprise, Zoho Recruit can scale with your organization's needs.

Who Can Use Zoho Recruit?

  • Recruiters:
    Both in-house and agency recruiters can benefit from the features Zoho Recruit offers to simplify sourcing and managing candidates.
  • Small to Large Enterprises:
    Zoho Recruit offers solutions for companies of all sizes, adapting to their recruitment needs and helping them manage large-scale hiring.
  • HR Professionals:
    HR teams can streamline their recruitment efforts by using Zoho Recruit to manage candidate workflows, track progress, and optimize their hiring strategies.

    Conclusion

    Zoho Recruit is an excellent choice for companies looking to simplify their recruitment process, improve efficiency, and maintain better collaboration within their hiring teams.
